NCSU Digital Technologies Development Librarian: technical scenario prompt

Purpose of project

The purpose of this project was to reduce the amount of time for the creation of weekly reports for journals integrated with Dryad.

The problem to be solved:

  • 137 integrated journals received weekly update reports
  • Senior Curator manually gathered info for emails from spreadsheets
  • Took Senior Curator almost an entire day (about 8 hours)
  • Given heavy volume of submissions - a huge time investment!

The solution: automation to the rescue

  • Worked with Senior Curator to establish requirements
    • Examined current reports
    • Gathered info on current process
    • Decided that automatic emails should not be generated
